Number of Albums Reviewed Per Year Broken Down by Genre

For this visualization we wanted to see if there was any bias being shown by Pitchfork in terms of the shear volume of reviews. Publications tend to play to their readership. Pitchfork has made a name for itself covering independent music, but has expanded its purview in recent years.

As we can see Rock music has consistently dominated the site's reviewed content. This does remain consistent with the brand Pitchfork has cultivated as an indie darling. However, it does appear that the site has taken measures to combat criticisms that they focused too heavily on their niche. There is a clear positive trend to the amount of attention being paid to Pop/R&B, Electronic, and Rap music.
