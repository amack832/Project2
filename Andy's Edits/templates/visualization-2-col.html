Top Albums per Year

Overview

This graph shows the top albums for each year in our specified time period. Each year can be accessed by using the drop down arrow on the bottom left of the graph. Any album in that specific year that had a score greater than or equal to 9.0 was chosen.

Not only do these graphs show us the individual albums, but also the counts of scores 9.0 or higher. There was an average of roughly 18 songs that were given a score of 9.0 or higher during this time period. 2005 held the highest number of albums with a 9.0, with a count of 28 hits.
