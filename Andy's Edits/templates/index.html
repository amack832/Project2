Objective

For our project we used two datasets that contain music albums with ratings and metdata (such as genre, artist, and year). Our intent was to compare the datasets by looking at trends, and perform compare and contrasts.

The primary dataset that we used is from Pitchfork, which is an online music publication that was founded in 1955. While the primary focus of the dataset is on independent music, it does include mainstream.

The second dataset we used is from Rolling Stone's top 500 albums. Rolling Stone is a music publication that was founded in 1967. The music in this dataset is mostly mainstream.
