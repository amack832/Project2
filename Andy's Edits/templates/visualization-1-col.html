View Genres Over Time

Overview

This graph shows the changes of average score of each genre per year. Those different genres can be accessed by using the drop down arrow on the bottom left of the graph.

Folk/country saw a significant drop off of average score after 1999.

Pop/R&B saw an increase in it’s average score after it’s lowest average score in 2003.

Rap saw a gradual negative regression during this time period.

Jazz saw no real significant change in average score.

Global dropped to a consistent average score of 7 after dropping to that score in 2000.

Electronic’s average score changes periodically over this time period, showing no real positive or negative regression.
